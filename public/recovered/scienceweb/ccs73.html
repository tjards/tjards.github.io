    Comet Hyakutake

    On March 26, 1996 I went outside to see the new comet.
    When I looked up I could see it right away.
    It was right beside the Big Dipper, a little to the left.
    When I saw it through my neighbour's binoculars I focused a little and it was spinning clockwise or counterclockwise.
    It was white and I didn't see a tail, but my friend did.
    It looked like it was a star that was really foggy.

    Travis Jardine,Grade 6 J.Muise
